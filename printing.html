Cutting-Edge Machines for the Printing Sector

  • Accuracy Level : ±10 µm/m
  • Efficient Clamping Mechanism using High Power Electromagnets
  • Plasma Cutting and straightening raw material to reduce cost
  • Non-Destructive Testing
  • CMM – FARO Arm for precision measurement

We provide precision CNC machining solutions for the printing and packaging sector, ensuring accuracy, durability, and cost-effectiveness. With advanced clamping mechanisms, plasma cutting, and specialized coatings, our components are engineered to enhance performance and reliability in printing operations.

Inder Enterprises was established in 1982 with an aspiration of becoming a frontrunner in precision manufacturing industries. The business was directed by Mr Inderjeet Singh Rao (Director) and Mr Chiranjeev Singh Rao (Executive Director).
